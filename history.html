Our History

Samuel Aderoyeje started his journey through his desire to see his peers acheive greatness. This desire led him to encourage the people around him into their untapped potential. He first started by advising his peers on social media platforms with techniques to further promote their project and that then progressed to the entrepreneurs trusting Sam with their whole social media pages. Sam would then create campaigns through innovative ideas, starting new trends and leading the marketing in whatever direction he chooses.

From the tender age of 17, Sam has been working with numerous musicians and various clothing brands with the same goal in mind. Growth! Sam's passion to see what people can do when the opportunity arises has pushed him to create Ade Marketing LTD as he continues to put his all into seeing the best out of all that he works with. Providing new opportunities and breaking glass ceilings.

Since our inception, we have been on a steady growth trajectory. Driven by a passion for empowering black businesses and a commitment to delivering exceptional results, we have built a solid reputation within the community. Our initial focus was on building strong relationships and offering personalized consulting services to help businesses navigate the digital landscape.
