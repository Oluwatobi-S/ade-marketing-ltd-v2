About Us

Ade Marketing Ltd is dedicated to empowering and supporting up-and-coming black businesses in the UK. We understand the unique challenges that black entrepreneurs face, and we are committed to providing tailored marketing solutions that help these businesses thrive and succeed.


Our team of experienced professionals offers a wide range of services, including brand development, digital marketing, social media management, and content creation. We work closely with our clients to understand their goals and develop strategies that deliver measurable results.


At Ade Marketing Ltd, we believe in the power of community and collaboration. Our mission is to elevate black-owned businesses, helping them to reach their full potential and make a lasting impact in their industries.
